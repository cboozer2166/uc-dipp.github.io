About Data in Public Policy

Data in Public Policy (DIPP) is a student organization started to build analytical skills and expertise among Harris students in open-source data platforms, foster a professional relationship with the data and tech community in Chicago, and to generally serve as a resource for Harris students interested in the intersection of data and public policy.
